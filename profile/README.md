## About DigiMeld

DigiMeld UG (haftungsbeschränkt) develops and operates the **MELD® Ecosystem** — a growing family of digital products, software platforms and shared technical infrastructure built on a common engineering foundation.

The ecosystem currently consists of **26 projects**, including production applications, SaaS solutions, internal platform services and reusable infrastructure components. Some projects intentionally combine multiple closely related applications and services under a single product name.

Our long-term focus is to build reliable backend systems, APIs and platform modules that work consistently together, remain maintainable over time and provide a stable foundation for future products.

---

## Shared Platform

The MELD® Ecosystem is built upon shared infrastructure, including:

- AI routing and orchestration
- Communication and notification services
- Geolocation and verification systems
- Identity and trust services
- Billing and accounting infrastructure
- Shared APIs and backend modules

This common technical foundation enables specialized products without duplicating the same core functionality across multiple independent systems.
